Our Process

With Our Processing Flow

Steam Generation

Steam generation is a crucial part of our processing flow. We utilize state-of-the-art steam generators to ensure optimal production conditions. Our steam generation process is highly efficient and eco-friendly.

Power Transmission

Power transmission is the final step in our energy management process. We ensure reliable transmission of power throughout our facility to drive various machinery and equipment. Our power transmission systems are designed for maximum efficiency and minimal energy loss.

Power Generation

Our power generation process involves the efficient conversion of steam energy into electrical power, allowing us to operate our mills smoothly and sustainably. We employ advanced technologies to optimize power generation and reduce environmental impact.

Our Rice Milling Flow

Grading

Grading is the initial step in our rice milling process, where we sort the rice grains based on size and quality. This ensures uniformity and consistency in the final product. Our grading process is precise and ensures only the highest quality grains are selected.

Cleaning

Cleaning involves removing impurities, such as dust, husk, and other foreign particles, from the rice grains to enhance their purity and quality. Our cleaning process is thorough and ensures the rice grains are free from any contaminants.

Milling

Milling is the core process where the rice grains are processed to remove the outer layers and produce polished rice ready for consumption. Our milling process is carefully controlled to produce rice with the perfect texture and flavor.
