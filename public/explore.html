Our History

Sri Lalitha Rice Mill was established in 1985 with a vision to produce the finest quality rice. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading rice mills in the region, known for our commitment to quality and excellence. Our journey began with a small mill, and today we operate a state-of-the-art facility equipped with the latest technology.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of premium quality rice, ensuring customer satisfaction through innovation, quality, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To produce and supply the highest quality rice products while maintaining sustainable practices, fostering community development, and continuously improving our processes to meet the needs of our customers.

View of Success

At Sri Lalitha Rice Mill, we define success not only by our financial achievements but also by the positive impact we make on our community and environment. We believe that success is measured by the satisfaction of our customers, the well-being of our employees, and our contribution to sustainable development.
