Idly Rice

About Idly Rice

Idly Rice is a variety of rice specifically used for making idlis, a popular South Indian dish. It has a high starch content, which aids in fermenting the idli batter effectively. Idly Rice is known for its smooth texture and is a staple in South Indian cuisine.

Additional Information:

  • Origin: South India
  • Texture: Smooth
  • Best for: Idlis, steamed cakes
